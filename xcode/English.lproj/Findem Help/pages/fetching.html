Fetching new lyrics for a song in Findem

This will show you how to try and automatically find lyrics for a song with Findem. This assumes you have added songs to Findem already; and you know how to use the panel.

To find lyrics for a song

  1. Click on the Lyrics tab
  2. Click on the Fetch Lyrics Button (if no lyrics are found, try Google Search below)
  3. Click Apply to apply the lyrics to all the songs in the panel







To find lyrics using Google

  1. Click on the Lyrics tab
  2. Click on the Google Search Button
  3. Once you find the lyrics you want, drag and drop them into the window
  4. Click Apply to apply the lyrics to all the songs in the panel
