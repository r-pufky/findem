Automatically Formatting Lyrics

Findem will automatically try to format the lyrics, correcting common mis-spellings, as well as fixing capitalization issues. This assumes you have lyrics to format.

To format lyrics for a song

  1. Verify that there is some text in the textbox
  2. Click on Format
