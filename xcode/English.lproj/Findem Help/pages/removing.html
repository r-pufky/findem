Removing songs from Findem

To remove songs that you do not want Findem to process (or songs that you accidently added by mistake), follow these steps:

To remove songs from the Findem queue

  1. Click on the Songs Queue tab in Findem
  2. Select a track that you want to remove
  3. Click on the - button in the lower right






To remove individual songs from the Findem queue

  1. Click on the Lyrics tab in Findem
  2. Click on the song's Album in the right panel
  3. Click on the - button in the lower right
